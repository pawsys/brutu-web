About

Brutu is an iOS timer that helps you stay focused. Select any amount of money that motivates you and start a timer. Put your phone down and stay on task. If you leave the app Brutu will charge you. You can still receive phone calls or swipe to the notification center. You can also add a break to your timer - leave the app and Brutu will notify you when to come back.
